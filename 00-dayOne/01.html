First Day Notes:

NOTES

  • When using Emmet, class is notatated by a "." and id is by "#".
  • HTML stands for hypertext markup language.
  • Doctype states the type of our document. In this case HTML.
  • our html tag denotes our language to be english, per lang="en" .
  • The head tag houses metadata, or detailed data about our doc.
  • UTF-8 specifies character encoding. Each character (letter, number, even spaces) has a numeric value.
  • viewport notes the device we are reading our document on.
  • title reflects the name that displays on our browser tab.
  • link allow us to connect to other files, such as our stylesheet (CSS).
  • a style tag can help us inject css into our document. *Not the best practice though
  • the body tag houses all the other tags that we want to display onto our browser. This wll be what the user sees!
